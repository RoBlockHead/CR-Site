Outreach Efforts

Learn more about what our team has been up to.

Project Drive Forward

Drive Forward aims to educate our local community about robotics and inspire the next generation to participate, reaching out to many different audiences. In the past 3 years, CircuitRunners has reached nearly 27,000 people at 61 different events throughout metro-Atlanta area. Read more here!

Posted 10/24/2017

Going Global

CircuitRunners has expanded our outreach efforts to a global scale. We mentor multiple teams from around the world, are assisting in the creation of FRC and FTC competitions in the Carribean and Africa, and helping international teams finance their trip costs. Read more here!

Posted 10/30/2017

AIM2LEAD

CircuitRunners partners with United Technologies, Chick-Fil-A, and General Electric to teach students about life in a STEM workplace and leadership skills. Read more here!
