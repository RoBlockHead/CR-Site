Teams

FRC

GRITS is a Georgia off-season FRC competition that uses the previous year's FRC challenge.

The First Tech Challenge is a yearlong alliance competition with tight size requirements and predetermined materials.

BEST Robotics makes students get creative with unconventional materials and interesting rules.
