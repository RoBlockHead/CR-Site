Pay CircuitRunners Dues

On this webpage you can pay your $150 September dues. These dues go towards getting your club merchandise package (club shirt, hoodie, hat. etc), purchasing/maintaining tools, registering the competition teams, and getting robot materials. An additional $4.65 is added as a service fee for paying dues this way. Dues can also be paid by submitting a check made out to "CircuitRunners Robotics, Inc." with your student's name in the memo. Checks are only able to be collected at the front office of Wheeler High School.
