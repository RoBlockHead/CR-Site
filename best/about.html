The CircuitRunners

Mission

The CircuitRunners are dedicated to making the future innovators that will drive tomorrow.

Our mission is to inspire young people to be science and technology leaders, by engaging them in exciting mentor-based programs that build science, engineering and technology skills, that inspire innovation, and that foster well-rounded life capabilities including self-confidence, communication, and leadership. BEST Motto: “Inspiring students to pursue careers in engineering, science, technology, and mathematics through robotics design…”
