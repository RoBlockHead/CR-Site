Our Leadership

Emily Johnson BEST lead

A 4th year employee who oversees the CircuitRunners BEST division and the completion of all tasks and deliverables for the team.

Yu Chang and Praveen Doluweera Build Co-leads

Yu and Praveen are both 3rd year employees who oversee the management, manufacturing and fabrication of the product. By applying real life engineering design processes and methods, these two, along with their assigned employees, are responsible for supplying the demand of the customers and creating a product that will be able to accomplish these demands.

Jessie Liu Notebook Lead

A 3rd year employee who oversees the engineering project documentation and notebook. She and her employees ensure the entire robot design and building process is properly documented through the use of meeting notes, sketches, CAD drawings, and daily agendas.

Jonathan Paul Presentation Lead

A 3rd year employee who oversees the marketing of our product. He and his employees are responsible for creating a marketing presentation that adequately conveys the various aspects of our organization and our product.

Carter Davis Booth Lead

A 2nd year employee who oversees the creation of an company exhibit booth. He and his employees design a booth that is informative and aesthetically appealing to explain our company and product effectively.
