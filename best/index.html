The CircuitRunners BEST Team

2016 BEST Season competition

On November 5th, our team competed at the Georgia Hub competition.

Our robot was named Innovative Design to Generate Advanced Farms, or IDGAF for short.

CircuitRunners obtained the BEST Award this year at the Hub competition as well as the Glenn Allen Award for our mentor, Ramana Divakaruni, as well as the Roy Al George Electronics Award.

Build team

Our build team is broken down into two divisions: manufacturing and robot design. Manufacturing works on creating parts for the construction of the robot as well as practice game parts. The build team is responsible for the assembling the robot.

Presentation team

Our presentation team gives a marketing presentation to a panel of judges and is tasked with selling our robot, the “product”.

Notebook team

Our notebook team created a compilation of all the tasks and activities that go into creating the robot. The entire season was documented through this notebook including brainstorming, research, etc.

Booth team

The booth team is responsible for designing a booth that represents our team and the theme of the competition this year, centralized around agriculture.
