HTML Standards

Below is the complete list of topics that will be tested as part of the HTML sections of the written test and team challenge. Links to study these topics are also included.

However, we recommend that new students start by working through the Mozilla Development Network's HTML, CSS, and JavaScript modules. People with no knowledge of the web should also complete MDN's Web module. By completing these modules in their entirety, students will receive a more complete learning experience than if they focused on only the topics outlined below. Students should instead use the standards as a way of checking that they understand the important information and brushing up on hazy topics.

Remember, if you are having any trouble with the content, please don't hesitate to reach out to the C3 team for help here.
